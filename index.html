Gone but Never Forgotten

One of my favorite sites I used to visit as a kid got shut down the other day. I figured why not reflect on some of my favoirte childhood memories. Rip cartoonnetwork.com.

Millsberry Coconut M&Ms Blockbuster Field Day Book Fair Libby Lu Scooby Doo Fruit Snacks

Millsberry

I still dream about this site. 💫 Unfortunately, this website is completely gone. No evidence of it existence remains beyond the screenshots that float around on reddit. Not even the best hackers on the planet can bring it back. *sigh* Its been gone for over a decade and still pains me to this very day. 😔 Millsberry walked so sites like Webkinz, Club Peguin and Roblox could run.

Coconut M&Ms

The amount of times someone has gaslight me over the exisitance of this crazy.😒 As a former, and still current candy addcit, nothing has come close to this. I dont even want Mars to bring them back because I know the new reciepe will be disrepctuful and belong in the trash. 🗑🤢 Absolutely a 10/10 candy.🤩 AND I dont even like coconut. Do not bother mentioning mounds or almond joys those belong in the trash too.

Blockbuster

All I have to say is that I used to grab 5 movies as a kid for only $1 and get them for a week.😋 I dont get as much joy from opening Netflix or HBOmax as I did going to Blockbluster and renting my favorite Bratz or Barbie movies💅. No streaming apps to this day have a decent collection of 2000s movies/shows. Physical media forms are becoming more and more diffucult to obtain. RIP Blockbuster. You were a 💎.

Field Day

Literally the best day in the school year. Argue with the wall. 1000000000/10. 🌟🌟🌟🌟🌟 I dont know if these are still a thing anymore for kids. However, I would invent a time machine that youthens you to do kickball and relay races 🏃 one more time.

Book fair

Another favorite time at school, not as good as field day tho. I had an amazing 💯 easer collection and it would of never been possible without the Scholastic book fair and my parents' money. 🙌🎊💃The amount of dopamine book fairs produced should really be studied. 10/10.

Libby Lu

💕 I LOVED THIS PLACE.💕 I would spend hours here even if my parents didn't buy me anything. There was glitter EVERYWHERE. Huge fan of glitter. I really do miss seeing these types of stores for younger ages. This store was definitely more for girly-girls💅, but there were more stores around that time that catered to different personalities. You really don't see stores like this anymore.

Scooby Snacks

Last but certainly not least. They got rid of the opaque blue fruit snack.😑 Terrible call on their end. 🤦 Might as well just discontinue the whole product after that decision. The ONE THING👏 that made this snack GOD-TEIR👏, they got rid of. I still see people to this day post about it on social media. What a shame....
